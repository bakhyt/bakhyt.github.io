About Me

I am a PhD student specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), at the School of Computer Science, University of Birmingham. My research explores the topic: "An NLP-based Approach to Capturing Similarity of Text Documents for Central Asian Languages, with Kazakh as a Case Study". The goal of this work is to develop a plagiarism detection system specifically designed for Central Asian languages, with a particular emphasis on Kazakh.
